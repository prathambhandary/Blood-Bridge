🚨 The Reality We Face

    12,000+ people die every day in India due to blood shortages.
    Every 2 seconds, someone needs blood.
    1 in 3 people will need blood in their lifetime.

And yet, many willing donors don’t know where or how to help. We’re here to fix that.

💡 What Makes Us Different?

Blood Bridge is a Hyperlocal, real-time blood matching platform. We cut through red tape and delays by using your location to instantly connect donors and recipients when it matters most.

⚙️ How It Works

📍 Register with your blood group, pin code, and availability.
🔔 Get notified when someone nearby urgently needs your blood type.
🚑 Head to the hospital or blood bank. Save a life. It’s that simple.

❤️ Our Mission

To stop preventable deaths due to blood unavailability by building India’s strongest grassroots blood donor network — one drop, one hero, one life at a time.
