About Us

We are a student-led innovation team, passionate about integrating technology with real-life safety. Our Smart Health Band stores critical medical information accessible by scanning a QR code, enabling instant emergency assistance.

Our Vision

To create a safer world where individuals with health conditions are always supported during emergencies. Our goal is to equip every vulnerable person with an affordable, accessible tool that can speak when they can't.

The Idea

A comfortable wristband equipped with a unique QR code that links to a personalized health dashboard: medical conditions, allergies, emergency contacts, medications, and critical notes. Quick scan, quick help.
